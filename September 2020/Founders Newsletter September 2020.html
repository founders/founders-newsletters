Event Spotlight: 54 Startup Weekend

Every fall semester, Founders hosts the annual 54 Startup Weekend, a three day startup event where University of Illinois students, regardless of background or major, come together to launch their very own startup idea from scratch. Most importantly, no prior experience is required to attend 54 as the event is intended to teach you how startups work and how you can potentially create your own! By the end of the event, you will have pitched dozens of ideas, formed teams, and also get expert guidance on how to push your startup forward. We even provide cash prizes and networking resources to the teams with the best ideas for their startup! Free swag will be available despite the ongoing pandemic, so don’t wait!

Apply by September 30 so you can be a part of 54 Startup Weekend!
