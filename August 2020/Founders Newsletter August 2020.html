Founders Is Back!

We’ll be sending out a monthly newsletter during the school year, highlighting the latest in startups both on and off campus. Each month, we’ll have a highlight of a few student startups, a list of upcoming events, a few tips for students who are thinking about turning a project into a startup, and more!

Want to get involved? Student startup looking for something? Shoot us an email at team@founders.illinos.edu

This Newsletter consists of

  • Event Spotlight: 54
  • Recruiting
  • Bootcamp
 

Event Spotlight

54 is a 3-day startup event at UIUC aimed towards providing students with resources and mentorship to turn feasible ideas into entrepreneurial designs.

Throughout the weekend you will get the opportunity to hear from seasoned entrepreneurs in their experiences, practice your pitching skills, and get advice from mentors with real world entrepreneurial backgrounds. Returning for its eighth year, and in the midst of the Covid-19 pandemic, 54 will be held virtually via Zoom on October 9-11, 2020.

Applications for 54 are now open
